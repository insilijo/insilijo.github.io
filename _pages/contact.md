The best way to reach me depends on what you're writing about.

**Research, software, or collaboration** — email is best: [joseph.j.gardner@gmail.com](mailto:joseph.j.gardner@gmail.com). I'm happy to talk about metabolomics, network biology, multi-omics tooling, or anything in the insilijo stack.

**Professional inquiries** — [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/microbesinsilico/) works well for connection requests and professional context.

**Blog or writing** — questions and comments on specific posts are open via the Giscus thread at the bottom of each post. For longer exchanges, email is fine.

**Bugs, feature requests, or questions about the tools** — please open a GitHub issue in the relevant repository. That keeps the conversation public and findable for others with the same question.

I respond to most things, but not always quickly.
